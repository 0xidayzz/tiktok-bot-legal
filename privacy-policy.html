Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This application ("the App") is a personal, non-commercial tool used to generate and schedule short-form video content for a single TikTok account owned and operated by the developer.

Data we access

The App accesses the following data via the TikTok API, solely to publish video content to the developer's own TikTok account:

  • Basic profile information (open id, display name, avatar) via the user.info.basic scope.
  • Video upload/posting capability via the video.upload / video.publish scope.

Data storage

Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored locally on the developer's own private hardware and are never shared with, sold to, or transmitted to any third party. No TikTok user data is collected from any account other than the developer's own.

Data retention

Tokens are retained only as long as necessary to operate the App and are deleted upon request or when the App is discontinued.
